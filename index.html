FAQ

Questions parents ask us most.

When should my child have their first dental visit?
+
We recommend a first visit by their first birthday or within six months of their first tooth coming in.
Do you take dental X-rays?
+
Yes — digital X-rays only when needed, using the lowest radiation settings available for children.
Do you accept insurance?
+
We work with most major dental insurance plans and offer flexible payment options.
How often should my child see the dentist?
+
Every six months for a checkup and cleaning, or sooner if we recommend closer monitoring.
